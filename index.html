GAME RULES

You can spread the virus clicking a healthy man, but your target must be next to a Zombie and then, he will infect the same color around him.
TIP: If you get a spreading combo you can obtain bonus zombies and extra points. A bonus Zombie can infects multiple colors at the same time.
The objective is to convert all the victims in Zombies.
